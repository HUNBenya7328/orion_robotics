About Orion Robotics

The advance of technology is based on making it fit in so that you don't really even notice it, so it's part of everyday life.

— Bill Gates

Our Mission

At Orion Robotics, our mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators by making technology fun, hands-on, and accessible. We believe every student should have the opportunity to build, experiment, and dream about the future of robotics.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every classroom can empower students to think creatively, solve problems independently, and use technology for good. By blending education with innovation, we’re helping young minds shape tomorrow’s intelligent world.

Meet Our Team

Alex Morgan

Founder & CEO

Alex founded Orion Robotics with a simple goal — to make robotics education engaging and accessible for all students. With over 10 years of experience in educational technology, he leads the company’s vision and innovation.

Sophia Lee

Lead Robotics Engineer

Sophia designs the hardware behind our robots. Her passion for engineering and her background in robotics competitions drive Orion’s technical excellence.

Michael Torres

Director of Education

Michael works with teachers and schools to create meaningful learning programs around Orion robots. He ensures that our technology truly enhances education.

Innovation at Orion

From the very beginning, Orion Robotics has been about more than just machines — it’s about sparking curiosity. We continuously develop new learning kits, coding challenges, and interactive materials that help students understand the “why” behind technology. Our research team explores AI-driven learning tools, ensuring our robots evolve alongside the students who use them.
